How We Make Premium Potato Flakes

Agriculture

Our journey begins on trusted farms where potatoes are grown with care, ensuring ideal texture & natural taste.

Fresh Potato Selection

We source only the finest potatoes from verified farms. Stored in controlled facilities for year-round freshness.

Washing

Potatoes go through high-pressure water jets to remove impurities. Only the cleanest potatoes move ahead.

Peeling

Potatoes are gently peeled without damaging the inside. Peels are repurposed as cattle feed‚Äîzero waste.

Steam Boiling

Peeled potatoes are steamed under high pressure, cooked evenly while locking nutrients without excess water.

Mashing

Cooked potatoes are mashed into a smooth, uniform texture. Natural additives ensure consistent quality.

Drying Process

Advanced dryers gently remove moisture, forming golden flakes with reliable performance & perfect texture.

Final Potato Flakes

The final flakes are inspected, packed in food-grade bags, and shipped globally by Shiv Shree Food.
